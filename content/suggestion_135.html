Adios Caffeine!

Addicted to caffeine and looking for a healthier substitute without compromising on that instant boost of energy??

Here is a list of our top 5 substitutes to help you put away that cup of coffee.

1.Ayurvedic Roast

This has some interesting grains and herbs that make for a wonderful warm beverage. This one is 100% certified organic too. Barley, rye, chicory root, Ashwagandha (this is an anti-oxidant that boosts the immunity system and calms the mind), Shatavari (shares a lot of the positive qualities that Ashwagandha does), and also Brahmi (which helps maintain mental clarity) make up this unique formula. You have a choice of 4 ways to brew this organic coffee alternative: boil it, use a regular drip coffee maker, a coffee or tea press or espresso machine.

2.Teeccino

This is a good herbal coffee made with barley, chicory root, figs, almonds and roasted carob. This coffee substitute contains no soy or caffeine and is gluten free. They have many different flavors, which gives a wide variety to match any mood. Their original flavor has a touch of citrus from orange peel additions, Mocha, Almond Amaretto, Vanilla Nut, Hazelnut and Chocolate Mint are just to name a few. They are sold in individual packs, 8.5 ounces and 5 lb bags.

3.Dandy Blend

This is an instant coffee substitute. Dandelion root is its main ingredient, a highly respected healing herb. Contains no caffeine and not bitter tasting. Gluten free and acid free. A delicious cup of Dandy Blend is zero points according to the Weight Watchers program, only 5 calories per cup. Also comes in individual packs to take with you everywhere.

5.Cafix

This is old standyby offers a nice hot drink that will make you feel relaxed and refreshed. The raw ingredients are malted barley, chicory, figs and beetroots which are ground together to give Cafix its unique flavor. No fat or cholesterol is included with Cafix, plus low calories (only 5 per serving), low sugar, and low carbohydrates (just ONE) makes this a guilt free beverage. This instant drink is enjoyed by young people without the worry of them being hyper active and by adults alike.
