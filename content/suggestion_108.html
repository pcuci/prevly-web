A Smooth(i)e start to the day!

Start your morning off right with a delicious dose of goodness.

These recipes help curb your cravings and stabilize your blood sugar levels, leaving you feeling fuller for longer.

1.Blueberry Basil Smoothie

Ingredients

1 cup blueberries

1/4 cup basil leaves

1/2 cup spinach leaves

1 frozen banana

2 cups unsweetened almond milk

Blend ingredients until smooth, smile, sip, and enjoy!

Nutritional info per serving:

249 calories, 6g total fat, 50g carbohydrates, 9g fiber, 28g sugar, 5g protein

2. Green Monster Juice

Ingredients

1 cup pineapple

1 apple

4 large kale leaves

1/2 bunch of parsley

1 cucumber

6 large stalks of celery

1 knob of ginger

Blend ingredients until smooth, smile, sip, and enjoy!

Nutritional info per serving:

226 calories, 2g total fat, 35g carbohydrates, 15g sugar, 5g fiber, 8g protein

3. Go-to-green juice

Ingredients

1 cup raw baby spinach

1/2 green apple, with skin

1 kiwi, peeled

3-inch cucumber piece

1 tbs lemon juice

splash of water (as needed)

1/2 cup lemon-flavored sparkling water or seltze, chilled

Blend ingredients until smooth, smile, sip, and enjoy!

Nutritional info per serving:

95 calories, 23g carbohydrates, 5g fiber, 2g protein
