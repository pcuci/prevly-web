Be ready when hunger hits!

Stock your pantry for smart bites between meals.

When you’re hungry it’s easy to mindlessly grab a handful of chips or cookies or some other snack filled with fat and sugar.

With a decently stocked pantry and refrigerator, and a couple of extra minutes, you can create easy, guilt-free snacks and mini-meals that are both satisfying and good for you. Here are five of our favorites.

1.Quick Trail Mix

Mix equal parts semi-sweet chocolate chips, dried cranberries and your choice of roasted nuts (hazelnuts, peanuts, almonds, cashews, walnuts, pecans).

2.Yoghurt Parfait

Top ½ cup of Greek yogurt with ½ cup prepared seasonal fruit, drizzle with agave or honey and sprinkle with 2 teaspoons chopped roasted nuts.

3.Almond and Cream Cheese stuffed Colossal Olives

Scrape a roasted almond tip across a bar of light (Neufchatel) cream cheese to pick up a generous ¼ teaspoon. Stuff it, cream cheese end down, into a colossal pitted olive. (Trim olive base if you want it to stand up.)

4.Goat Cheese Topped cherry tomatoes

Halve grape or cherry tomatoes lengthwise and lightly sprinkle with salt and pepper. Smear each half with a generous ¼ teaspoon of fresh goat cheese.

5.Salsa Deviled Egg

Halve a boiled egg and remove yolk. Mash yolk with a tablespoon of your favorite salsa or salsa verde. Spoon filling back into egg halves and enjoy. If there’s time, top eggs with minced sun dried tomato.
