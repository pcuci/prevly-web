What is Prevly?

Prevly empowers people to live better and healthier lives. We believe that the best form of preventative medicine are short-term decisions that uphold one's health and well-being, day in day out.

We incorporate predictive intelligence and data-driven contextual triggers to send you health notifications and reminders throughout the day and provoke healthier actions.

Best of all, Prevly is designed to be super-personalized. What does this mean? All the advice you get is adapted to your lifestyle and health needs.

We’ve worked with healthcare practitioners and behavioral scientists to deliver you wellness advice backed by science, wherever you go.
