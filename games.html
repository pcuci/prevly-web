You're in good hands

The micro-social real-life games are passionately brought to you by a team of grunts who care deeply about preventing the onset of chronic metabolic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes in the broader population.

Prevly is the result of two years of intense collaboration and experimentation between Shawn and Paul, and later Vish and Seb, whom we joined forces with this summer. Founded May 2014, at Prevly we want to make taking care of your health easy and fun for everyone.
