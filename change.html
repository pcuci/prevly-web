About Us

At SANA, we strive to empower people to live better and healthier lives. We believe that the best form of preventative medicine is the culmination of short-term decisions that uphold one's health and well-being, day in day out.

To this end, we are developing a technological platform that incorporates predictive intelligence and data-driven contextual triggers to send mobile-based notifications and reminders to users throughout the day that provoke realtime actionable steps towards better health.

Our technology is a convenient and accessible means of ensuring that the importance of health and well-being is not diminished amidst the chaotic flow of our daily lives.

Well-being and health are entities deeply embedded within the fabrics of context. We recognize the dynamic nature of such entities, and take a holistic approach in addressing them on the realms of both the physical and mental.

Given these intricacies, we are developing highly-interactive and diversified means of engaging users, with the aims of leveraging self-empowerment as a path towards behaviour modification.
